Craig Fyfe

Bird cage

2015

Part of an ongoing project named 'Punch Line'

This project was started after I got a bad Critic from a guest lecture at Chelsea Collage of Arts, I had started this on going project where I was told that my 'one liners' where boring and to easy to understand making them uninteresting, I completly disagree as I have stood at many a deep pice of work and realised it meant something different to me than it did the Artist that produced it, I like the straight fowardness and honesty in the works that I did, I knew people would understand and that conncection was important in these pieces, I wanted to make a quick joke, a breath in a normlay stiffeling world. 

To create a smile
