Aftermath

2004

Filled my room with balloons, nearly killed myself blowing them up manually, then popped them all. 

Continuing with the busy study time, I often had shows upcoming, without any material to actually show. So I aimlessly went out of my way to make things, unmake and remake them, to end up with nothing to show but me, keeping myself busy with work, without knowing why or what I was creating.
